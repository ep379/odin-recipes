Cheese Pastelito

Description

This beef empanada recipe is made with Goya Discos pastry rounds and a mouthwatering filling of Tropical's "Queso De Freir" Cheese. Enjoy these flaky, deep-fried empanadas as an appetizer, a main dish, or even after your main course.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Goya Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4 Servings Of Tropical Queso De Freir
  • Goya Discos Empanada Discs, thawed
  • 1 Quart Corn Oil, for frying

Steps

  1. Shred Cheese the appropriate sizing to comfortably fit the discs.
  2. Roll empanada disks on a lightly floured surface until 1/2 inch larger in diameter. Spoon about 1 tablespoon shredded cheese into the middle of each disk. Moisten the disk edges with water, fold in half over filling to form a half-moon, and pinch to seal (or seal with a fork)
  3. Heat 2 1/2 inches corn oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Working in batches, fry empanadas until crisp and golden brown, flipping once, 4 to 6 minutes. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
